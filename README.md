## 🎯 The Mission

> **40 million people** run India's hotels and restaurants.
> Almost none of them have ever had access to practical, job-ready training in
> their own language.

Global hospitality training is built for five-star chains and English-speaking
managers. **Bharat** — the cooks, stewards, housekeepers and front-desk staff of
Tier 2 and Tier 3 India — was left behind.

**Devo Edutech exists to change that.** Through our platform **Hotelwaley**, we
deliver short, mobile-first, multilingual training that workers can actually use
on the floor — turning an unserved workforce into a skilled one.

## 👤 The Founder

> _"Sometimes all it takes is vision, leftover tiles, and a lot of guts."_
> — **Ajay Handa**, Founder & Managing Director

In the 1990s, Ajay was building a bar inside his family's hotel in Hubli and ran
out of money before he could hire a bartender. So he drove **400 km** to
Bangalore, spent days shadowing the city's best bartenders, came home, trained
two local boys, and opened **The Zebra Crossing** — North Karnataka's first youth
nightlife venue.

Three decades later, the same instinct drives Devo Edutech: _no one was building
for Bharat's hospitality workforce, so he did._
